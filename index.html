Game Overview

Saves Enabled

Monster Survivors is an exciting survival game where you must fight against endless waves of monsters. Use your skills and strategy to survive as long as possible in this thrilling adventure.

With simple controls and addictive gameplay, Monster Survivors is perfect for quick gaming sessions or extended play. Each run is unique, with different upgrades and challenges. How long can you survive?

New Feature: Save Game Progress

Now you can save your game progress and continue your adventure later! Use the SAVE GAME button to store your current progress, and LOAD GAME to resume from where you left off. Auto-save can also be enabled to automatically save your progress every 5 minutes.

How to Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Automatically attack nearby monsters
  • Collect experience points to level up
  • Choose new abilities and upgrades as you level
  • Save your progress to continue later!
  • Survive as long as possible!

Game Features

  • Multiple character classes with unique abilities
  • Dozens of powerful upgrades and items
  • Varied monsters with different attack patterns
  • Dynamic difficulty scaling
  • Beautiful pixel art graphics
  • NEW: Save game progress system

Tips and Strategies

Beginner Tips

If you're new to Monster Survivors, focus on movement and avoiding enemies first. Keep moving in circular patterns to avoid getting surrounded.

Choose defensive upgrades early until you get comfortable with the game mechanics. Health regeneration and movement speed are excellent starting choices.

TIP: Save your game frequently when you've made good progress!

Advanced Strategies

For experienced players, focus on building synergistic abilities. Combine area damage with movement speed for a hit-and-run strategy.

Alternatively, focus on high damage and health regeneration for a tank build. Remember that positioning is key in the later waves when monsters become more numerous and aggressive!

PRO TIP: Create multiple save files to experiment with different builds!
